Can I take my dog in a taxi? Is it allowed, or do I need to mention this somewhere?

Inform the taxi driver about bringing a dog by adding a note in the special instructions, mentioning the dog's size. For cross-border trips, extra rules may apply. Contact the Service Center to ensure your booking includes dog transport.

Can I bring my wheelchair? Are your vehicles Wheelchair Accessible?

Most of our vehicles can accommodate a foldable wheelchair. Please consider the space it takes in the trunk along with your luggage. Mention it in the 'Special Instructions' before confirming your booking. For fully accessible vehicles, look for the ramp symbol. If you need one, be sure to specify it in the 'Special Instructions' before confirming your booking.

22 Sep

For an airport pickup, how far after I land should I ask to get picked up?

We suggest a pickup time for your convenience. If you have luggage, book 30-40 minutes after landing, or 20 minutes if you have only a carry-on. For international flights, add 30-60 minutes for customs. Call the driver once you land to let them know you’ve arrived.
